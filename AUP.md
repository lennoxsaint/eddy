By using Eddy you agree to the following.

1. **Footage rights.** You will only process footage you own or have the explicit right to edit.
   You will not process footage that infringes others' copyright, privacy, or publicity rights.

2. **People and likeness.** The thumbnail step sends selected face frames to a cloud image model
   which may alter likenesses. You will not use Eddy to create deceptive, defamatory, or
   non-consensual depictions of any person, and you are responsible for consent where required.

3. **Prohibited content.** You will not use Eddy to produce or process content that is unlawful,
   that sexualizes minors, that harasses or incites violence, or that violates the terms of any
   configured cloud provider.

4. **AI-generated disclosure.** Titles and thumbnails are AI-generated and may be inaccurate; you
   are responsible for reviewing them and for any disclosure required by the platforms you publish to.

5. **Your own keys and costs.** Cloud editorial and thumbnail steps run on credentials you provide
   and incur costs on your accounts. You are responsible for those costs and for complying with each
   provider's terms.

6. **No circumvention.** You will not use Eddy to bypass a platform's rules, rate limits, or
   content policies.

Violation of this policy may terminate your license (see EULA.md).
